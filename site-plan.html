Overview

Purpose

My website will be a website for people who wants to study better. It will contain many tips on how to study more effectively. Do you remember the last time you had a difficult test? Did it go well? Was your preparation effective? Studying does not need to be a struggle. Through many years of studying “how to study” and by trying to apply many study techniques I discovered how to study more effectively. I want to help you and everyone to study better and get better results from it. Because of this, I created this website where I share tips that will make your studying time more effective!

Audience

The main audience of the website will be people who are looking to improve their study habits, which comprises: students preparing for exams like the SAT or ENEM, students on high school on high school or university looking for better grades, students who are learning a new language, anyone who is looking for study tips.

Typography

Heading Font: Montserrat

Montserrat is a font that looks modern and elegant, which is perfect for the website purpose as it will focus on both young people and families. This font is also easily readable and is a perfect match for headings.

Paragraph Font: Sanchez

I choose this font as it combines with Montserrat in a great way. It is distinct to Montserrat, it is a bit more formal which is appropriate for a study website. I also chose to use this font because it is a serif font. There is an evident contrast between them.

Content

Home page

It is not important to sudy hard but to study smart. Studying smarter is the best way to achieve better results from your studies. Welcome to Study Smarter! Here you can learn everything about how to study on a smarter way. To study smarter you need to understand about the learning, retention and masterization processes. Study Smarter will help you to learn tips and tricks to maximize the outcomes from your studies. You may be asking yourself, why should I study about how to study? Well, there are many reasons to do so! First: by undersanding how to study you will be able to learn more information from your studies, keep more information and be able to reacess such information easily. By doing so, you will not need to study harder are doing to get better results, you only need to study smarter. Second: by studying smarter, you are going to get better results. It has been scientifically comproved that students who have better study methods achieve more than students who only study for longer hours. When I was a high school student, I constantly struggled with the feeling of needing to study more, so I constantly sacrified all other things for the sake of getting better grades. However, because I learned how to study better, now I can get even superior results AND studying for less time. Third: studying smarter will help you retain what you've studied. It is easy to forget. Every time I was learning a new language, it wasn't easy to memorize all the new words to use them on conversations. By learning the words and using them repeatedly I was able to really learn them. Doesn't the same apply to all the other kinds of knowledge? I know that you also forgot that important math formula during a test. So, how do we remember things that we studied weeks, months or even years ago? The answer is simple! By studying smarter.

Topics

Navigate through the topics that will help you study better:

Setting Goals: What are you going to study today? How will this help you in the future? Understanding such questions is essential for helping you to develop study habits. Here you can learn the importance of setting goals for your studies. By setting goals you will be able to always move towards a porpuse, by doing so your studies will become more meaningful.

Planning Your Studies: Before even starting, it is important to understand how to effectively plan your studies according to your own goals. Planning your studies will help you to face the contents you need to study in a more effective way. Remember, if you don't plan how to achieve your goals, they likely are not going to happen.

When should I review it? Learning is important, but you cannot forget about remembering. Without reviewing what you learn, you will never memorize it. Learn more about memory curve and how to effectively plan to review.
